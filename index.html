Frequently Asked Questions

When is the event?

March 19-22, 2026 at 1337 Camp Gaming House in Waiblingen (near Stuttgart).

What equipment is provided?

High-end PCs with AMD Ryzen 7 5700X, NVIDIA RTX 3060Ti, and 240Hz BenQ Zowie monitors. Preferably bring your own peripherals (mouse, keyboard, headset).

Where can I find the rules?

For the latest rules (formats, maps, pick/ban, any changes), please check our Discord #rules channel: discord.gg/u8Ttp8frqU

Do I need a team for CTF?

No‚ÄîCTF uses a draft system to balance teams. Captains will pick players on the day.

What's the minimum age?

16+ (under 18 may need guardian consent depending on venue policy).

Where do I sign up?

Use the registration form on the Register page. Status updates will be posted in Discord.

Can I attend without playing?

There is a possibility for spectators. For details, check our Discord.

Is accommodation included?

Accommodation is available on-site at 1337 Camp with various room options. Book early as space is limited!

What's the skill level requirement?

Join Discord to find out details. For CTF there is a minimal threshold of knowledge and experience needed. All skill levels are welcome for Duel!

Will there be streaming/VODs?

Selected matches may be streamed on twitch.tv/qllancityseries. Links will be posted in Discord.

What about food and drinks?

1337 Camp has a kitchen and lounge area. There are also restaurants and shops nearby.
